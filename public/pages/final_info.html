Zero to Hero

If you're signed up for Zero to hero, make sure you double check the schedule again - your program starts at 15:00, so make sure you arrive early to complete your check in.

We added some more activities around your classes, to make sure that you have a clear idea of what's happening at all times:

  • Zero to Hero Orientation

    A short meeting before your classes start, to go over the schedule with you and answer any questions you may have about the event or the program.

  • Zero to Hero Meet & Greet

    A chill hangout with all the ZtH participants and some of our volunteers. We'll have some snacks and drinks, and get to know eachother. Our friendly volunteers will be there to help you get in the party mood for your first social.

  • Discovery Hour

    A dedicated time slot on Friday night with beginner friendly music, where everyone joins with the same goal - to meet and dance with as many people they don't know yet. Don't be shy to ask someone to dance!

We invite you to join as many of those activities as you can, to take the full advantage of the course.

Food & Drinks

Tap water in Slovenia is drinkable, and actually pretty good. The hotel even holds a certificate for it. We ask you to bring your own water bottles and refil them with tap water from the restrooms.

Other drinks are available at the bar, which is open:

  • Friday 08:00 - 23:00
  • Saturday & Sunday 11:00 - 23:00

They prepared some promotional prices for dancers:

  • water 0,75l bottle 4 eur,
  • cuba libre 10 eur,
  • gin tonic 10 eur.

You may bring your own food and drinks to the venue, but please do NOT place them inside the ballrooms. Any open drinks will be removed and thrown away, especially if found near DJ booth or other electronic equipment.

Food is available in the hotel lounge, but their restaurant is a bit slow, and cannot process such large amount of people eating at the same time. Please consider ordering via Wolt or Glovo, or visiting one of the restaurants/bars nearby. Note that most restaurants in the area are closed on Sundays, so make sure to check beforehand.
