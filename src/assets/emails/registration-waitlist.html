Registration on Waitlist

We have received your registration, but it has been placed on the waitlist. This could be because you registered without a partner or your partner has not submitted their registration yet.

If there are still open spots when we can either match you with a partner or receive your partner's registration, we will send you an acceptance email. The price for the event will be calculated at the time of acceptance.

If you registered solo, you can speed up the process by finding a partner to sign up with you. In this case, they should include your email in their registration form. You will then be removed from the waitlist immediately, provided there are still available spots.

We invite you to follow the updates on the website and the Facebook event.
